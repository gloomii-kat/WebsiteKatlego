Engineering Student & Maths Tutor

I'm an electrical engineering student with a genuine love for understanding how things work, from the logic inside a computer program to the forces acting on a structure. I approach problems with curiosity first, and I've found that the same mindset that makes a good engineer also makes a good teacher.

Alongside my studies, I tutor high school learners in Mathematics and Physical Sciences. I believe every student is capable of understanding these subjects, they just need someone patient enough to find the right explanation for them.

Outside of engineering and tutoring, I'm drawn to game development, and building things that people actually use. I'm looking for opportunities where I can grow technically, contribute meaningfully, and keep learning.
