My Teaching Approach

Understanding first.
Speed follows.

Memorising formulas gets you through one test. Understanding them gets you through your entire school career and beyond.

01

Diagnose the gap

Every first session starts with understanding where a learner actually is; not where the syllabus says they should be. We identify the real sticking point.

02

Build the foundation

Before moving forward, I make sure the underlying concept is solid. We work through explanations, examples, and analogies until it truly makes sense.

03

Practice with purpose

Guided problem-solving builds fluency. I select questions that target weak spots and increase in difficulty as confidence grows.

04

Review & reinforce

Each session ends with a quick recap and a small set of practice questions to consolidate. Progress is tracked across sessions.

Common Questions

Questions parents
usually ask.

How do I book a first session?▼

Send me a message via the contact page. Include your child's grade, the subject they need help with, and a rough idea of availability. I'll reply within 24 hours to confirm a first session.

What does a session cost?▼

Rates depend on grade level, subject, and whether the session is online or in-person. Contact me directly for a quote; I aim to keep pricing fair and accessible.

How many sessions does my child need?▼

It depends on the goal. For ongoing support, one or two sessions per week works well. For focused exam prep, an intensive block of daily sessions can be arranged. I'll give an honest assessment after the first session.

Do you provide materials or notes?▼

Yes; I prepare session materials, worked examples, and practice questions. For online sessions these are shared digitally. Learners should also bring their school textbooks and past papers.

Will I know how my child is progressing?▼

Absolutely. I give brief updates after each session and flag any areas of concern. Parents are always welcome to check in; I see the relationship with the family as part of the support.

What if my child misses a session?▼

Life happens. I ask for at least 24 hours notice where possible so we can reschedule. Repeated last-minute cancellations may affect availability.
